About the Project

Veloce is an innovative bike rental application that revolutionizes urban mobility. The app allows users to easily find, unlock, and rent bikes using their smartphones. With features like QR code scanning, real-time bike tracking, and seamless payment integration, Veloce provides a smooth and convenient bike-sharing experience.

Built with Flutter and Firebase, Veloce offers a modern, responsive interface with comprehensive features including bike availability tracking, rental history, user profiles, and push notifications. The app supports multiple bike models and provides detailed information about each bike's specifications and location.

Key Features

QR Code Scanning

Quickly unlock bikes by scanning QR codes or entering bike IDs manually.

Real-time Tracking

Track available bikes in real-time with integrated maps and location services.

Multiple Bike Models

Browse and rent from a variety of bike models including classic and new models.

Rental History

View complete history of all your bike rentals and riding statistics.

Smart Notifications

Receive alerts about rental confirmations, time reminders, and special offers.

User Profile

Manage your profile, payment methods, and preferences in one place.
